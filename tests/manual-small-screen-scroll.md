# Small-Screen Scroll Acceptance

1. Open `index.html` in a normal browser window.
2. Resize the browser to about `900x650`.
3. Confirm mouse wheel or trackpad scrolling moves the page vertically.
4. Confirm the simulation, graph, controls, roadmap button, and bottom controls are reachable.
5. Confirm the onboarding overlay is not permanently active and does not block wheel or touch scroll.
